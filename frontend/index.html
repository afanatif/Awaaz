Before you commit time, capital, or legal risk, Awaz validates claims against real public signals. One input in. Multi-agent verdict out — with source-by-source contradiction reasoning and a complete audit trail.

How to Use

1

Enter a Claim

Type a business claim in the input field (e.g., "investing in silver is a good idea") or hold the microphone to speak your claim.

2

Awaz's multi-agent pipeline processes your claim: Ingestion → Analyst → Strategist → Executor → Monitor. Each agent specializes in a specific task.

Get the Verdict

Receive a detailed verdict with source-by-source contradiction reasoning, action recommendations, and a complete audit trail.
